Skrillex

The King of Dubstep

Born on January 15 1988 in Los Angeles, California Skrillex aka Sonny Moore showed an interest in music from a very early age. His earliest memories of music include his parents listening to The Doors, and Sonny has described himself as that kid who would bang on pots and pans. As he grew up as a kid he moved around different schools and eventually turned to home schooling at age 14 due to bullying.

His first venture into his music career came when he reached out to Matt Good of From First to Last. After auditioning he was made lead vocalist for the music group. With the band, Sonny went on to release two albums, Dear Diary, My Teen Angst Has a Body Count in 2004 and Heroine in 2006. After several successful tours however, Sonny suffered from vocal issues and had to resign as a member of the band.

Beginning to work on his solo career, in 2008 Sonny Moore began to perform under the name Skrillex. On June 7th 2010 Skrillex released his first EP titled My Name is Skrillex as a free download. Later that year Skrillex began touring with Deadmou5 after being signed to Mau5trap Recordings which led to his second EP, Scary Monsters and Nice Sprites. Skrillex also went on to debut several songs including Reptile, a remix of Benny Benassi's Cinema and First of the Year (Equinox) which would be featured on his follow up EP to Scary Monster and Nice Sprites titled More Monsters and Sprites. On August 17th 2011 Skrillex announced his label OWSLA, whos first releases included successful artists Porter Robinson and Alvin Risk. In December 2011 Skrillex unveiled the EP Bangarang. Between then and 2014 Skrillex collaborted with a wide variety of artists including Korn and Kill the Noise, Damian Marley and Knive Party. On March 7th 2014 Skrillex released his most recent LP Recess.
